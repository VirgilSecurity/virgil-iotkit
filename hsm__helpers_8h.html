Parameters
[in]recipient_idPointer to recipient_id array. Cannot be NULL.
[in]recipient_id_szSize of recipient_id array.
[in]encrypted_data_szSize of encrypted data.
[in]encrypted_dataPointer to the encrypted data. Cannot be NULL.
[in]iv_dataPointer to the iv vector of encrypted data. Cannot be NULL.
[in]encrypted_keyPointer to the encrypted symmetric AES GCM key. Cannot be NULL.
[in]iv_keyPointer to the iv vector of encrypted symmetric AES GCM key. Cannot be NULL.
[in]hmacPointer to the authentication data. Cannot be NULL.
[in]public_keyPointer to the recipient public key. Cannot be NULL.
[in]public_key_szRecipient public key size.
[out]cryptogramPointer to the buffer for created cryptogram. Cannot be NULL.
[in]cryptogram_buf_szSize of buffer for created cryptogram. Cannot be 0.
[out]cryptogram_szPointer to size of created cryptogram. Cannot be NULL.
Returns
VS_CODE_OK in case of success or error code.
